// How private-sale escrow works

FOUR EVENTS. ONE RECORD.

Instead of relying on a listing, chat thread, and payment note that tell different stories, VX Pay binds the commercial terms and settlement events into one verification trail.

01

Define the deal

Record the parties, amount, item or service, condition, provenance, disclosures, delivery method, and release conditions.

02

Fund the agreement

The buyer funds according to the recorded terms before the seller ships, transfers, or begins the agreed work.

03

Deliver and inspect

The seller records delivery. The buyer reviews the goods or work during the inspection window written into the agreement.

04

Acknowledge and release

The buyer acknowledgment and payment release become timestamped events in the same tamper-evident record.

How does VX Pay work for a private sale?

The parties record the deal and release conditions, the buyer funds the agreement, the seller delivers, and the buyer acknowledgment and release event are added to the verification trail.

What does a VX Pay agreement cost?

A sealed agreement costs $7.99. VX Pay agreements are included while a Solo, Pro, or Enterprise plan is active. See the full SENT. pricing page for delivery proof and verified transfer coverage too.

Is VX Pay a bank or licensed escrow agent?

No. VX Pay creates proof of an escrow payment agreement and coordinates settlement conditions. It is not a bank, licensed escrow agent, money transmitter, or source of legal advice. Funds handling is performed by the connected payment provider under its terms.

What evidence should I add?

Add enough detail to make the transaction unambiguous: item identifiers, condition, provenance, disclosures, delivery requirements, inspection timing, acceptance criteria, and any evidence both parties are relying on.
