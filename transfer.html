⚠ Important — what this is and isn't
SENT Transfer creates cryptographic proof of a transaction and its agreed terms only. It is not a legal title transfer, not a substitute for any state-required form (such as a DMV title transfer or odometer disclosure), and not legal advice. You remain responsible for completing any forms required by law for your item. SENT seals what was agreed, its condition, and when — nothing more.
